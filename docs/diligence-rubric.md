# Diligence Rubric

Lore should turn token diligence into a repeatable rubric, not a loose pile of notes.

## Core categories

- Governance quality and who can actually change the system.
- Treasury runway or other funding durability.
- Unlock pressure and how concentrated that pressure is.
- Fee retention or another reason value might stay with the protocol.

## Scoring reminders

- A strong governance story does not cancel out bad unlock structure.
- Runway should be scored on credibility, not just size.
- Fee retention only matters if users still choose the product.

## Output

The final memo should show category scores, the reason behind each score, and the main unresolved question that could change the view.
